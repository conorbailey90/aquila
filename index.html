Comprehensive Eye Care Services

From routine eye examinations to specialized diagnostics, we provide complete optometric care under one roof.

Complete Eye Examinations

A comprehensive check of your vision and overall eye health, covering everything from sharpness and prescription accuracy to how your eyes work together and stay healthy inside and out.

  • • Visual acuity and refractive error assessment
  • • Binocular vision and eye coordination tests
  • • Internal and external eye health evaluation

Advanced Diagnostic Imaging

State-of-the-art technology for early detection and monitoring of eye conditions, including OCT scans, retinal photography, and visual field testing.

  • • OCT scanning for macular and glaucoma assessment
  • • Digital retinal photography and imaging
  • • Visual field perimetry testing

Contact Lens Services

Expert fitting and aftercare for all types of contact lenses, from daily disposables to specialist lenses for astigmatism and presbyopia.

  • • Comprehensive contact lens fittings and trials
  • • Toric lenses for astigmatism correction
  • • Multifocal and specialist lens options

Eye Condition Management

Diagnosis, monitoring, and management of common eye conditions including glaucoma, cataracts, macular degeneration, and diabetic eye disease.

  • • Glaucoma screening and monitoring
  • • Diabetic retinopathy assessment
  • • Age-related macular degeneration monitoring

Dry Eye Assessment & Treatment

Specialized evaluation and treatment options for dry eye syndrome, including meibomian gland assessment and personalized treatment plans.

  • • Comprehensive dry eye evaluation
  • • Meibomian gland dysfunction assessment
  • • Customized treatment and management plans

Expert Eyewear Dispensing

Professional guidance in selecting frames and lenses that suit your prescription, lifestyle, and aesthetic preferences, with precision fitting and adjustment services.

  • • Personalized frame selection and styling advice
  • • Advanced lens options including varifocals and coatings
  • • Professional frame adjustments and repairs

We also provide children's eye examinations, sports vision assessments, and urgent eye care for sudden vision changes or eye injuries.
